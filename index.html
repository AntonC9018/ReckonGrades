Calculate average grade

Shortcuts
1 through 0: Add a grade of 1 through 10 correspondingly (0 = 10)
Delete: Delete the student you are focused on (the last in case of no focus)
Space: Add a new student
Up-Arrow: Refocus to the next student (the first one if the end had been reached)
Down-Arrow: Refocus to the previous student (the last one if the first is under focus)
Enter: Download the spreadsheet with grades entered (also submit button)
